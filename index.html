About Me

Bashar Subeh has been a photographer since 2009 shooting weddings, graduation portraits, headshots, events, and capturing art. Bashar shoots with the best equipment and turns over your photos within a few days of your shoot. With age and other unforeseen circumstances memories may fade. Photos are your memories that can stay forever to tell your story to your loved ones in the future. I am a man of many stories and I would love to capture yours.

For a quote, please contact me at bsubeh@gmail.com
